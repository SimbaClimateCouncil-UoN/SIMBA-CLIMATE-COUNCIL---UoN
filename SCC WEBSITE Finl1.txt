  <main class="container content">
    <h1>About Simba Climate Council</h1>
    <section class="profile">
      <h2>Vision</h2>
      <p>To be a university-rooted hub leading creative, governance-based interventions for climate action at institutional, national, and global levels.</p>

      <h2>Mission</h2>
      <p>To engage students, faculty, and professionals in shaping evidence-based policies and dynamic strategies for climate change mitigation and adaptation through advocacy, well-designed research, and strategic collaboration.</p>

      <h2>What We Do</h2>
      <p>Simba Climate Council (SCC) focuses on building capacity in climate governance, developing policy proposals, partnering with government and NGOs, and implementing creative climate interventions like eco-art and hackathons. We aim to influence university curricula to include governance-focused climate content and to mobilize student–faculty–professional collaborations.</p>

      <h2>Membership</h2>
      <p>Membership is open to all University of Nairobi students with an interest in climate governance. Members are expected to participate in SCC programs and demonstrate commitment to evidence-based climate action.</p>
